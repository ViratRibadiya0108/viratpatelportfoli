Common questions

Things people usually ask.

A few of the questions that come up most often before a project starts. If yours isn't here, just ask.

How long does a typical project take? +
MVPs usually ship in 6–10 weeks. Larger SaaS or marketplace builds run 3–6 months. After the discovery call I'll send you a concrete timeline you can plan against.
Do you work with overseas clients? +
Yes — the majority of my work is for clients in the US, UK, Europe, Singapore and Australia. Async-first with weekly demo calls works well across timezones.
Can you handle design as well as development? +
Yes. I design in Figma and build to production. For larger brand systems I'll often bring in a specialist designer, but for product UI I handle it end-to-end.
What technologies do you specialize in? +
Flutter for mobile, Next.js + Node for web, Firebase or Postgres for backends, Shopify for commerce. Modern, scalable, well-supported — not whatever's hot this month.
Do you offer post-launch support? +
Yes — most clients move onto a monthly retainer after launch for bug fixes, performance work and shipping new features. Pricing depends on scope.
How do payments and contracts work? +
Fixed-scope projects are split into milestones with a deposit up front. Retainers are billed monthly. Standard freelance contract — happy to use yours if you have one.
